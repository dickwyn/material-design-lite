Why choose an orange?

Vitamin C

Oranges are rich in vitamin c and in fact which is used by our bodies to improved immunity to diseases

Recognizable

Unlike other fruits, oranges only come in one color making it easy to find

Calories

If you are on a strict diet, orange are the way to go as they have lesser calories than any other fruit in this price

Fiber

Compared to apples, oranges provide 2x more fiber per serving
